The Lone Star Difference

Aside from offering two-year degrees such as Associate Arts (AA) and Assosciate of Science (AS), Lone Star College is proud to announce flagship programs in Information Technology and Renewable Energy. These programs are dedicated to providing cutting edge coursework and experience to Houston's next generation of leaders.

Simply Look Up

With 5,848 high school graduates alone in the fall of 2020, Lone Star College has been the choice of area high school graduates. That is ten times the number of students choosing the University of Houston and six times as many who attended Texas A&M University! The need for innovation within our education system has lead us to this very point.

When building our 8th Lone Star College campus, we have been dedicated to the progression of learning environments. Using an innovative combination of lighter-than-air technology, the Airship campus has the ability to easily move about the Houston area, while never straying too far from home base -- George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH). The Airship even has the ability to operate safely during natural disasters, making this self-sufficient campus the ideal learning environment.

"Where better to learn about cloud based computing than in the clouds? I think it's a perfect metaphor for what we are trying to get across to our students"

- Chancellor Lonny Starr
